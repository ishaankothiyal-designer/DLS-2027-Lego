## Use this skill for

- type ramp interpretation
- hierarchy planning
- typography documentation
- font-family override review

## Workflow

1. Keep the ramp shared unless there is an explicit system decision otherwise.
2. Combine Typography with Theme fonts at the component layer.
3. Preserve the role difference between title, headline, paragraph, utility, and caption.

## Guardrails

- Do not hide brand fonts inside Typography.
- Do not collapse title into headline semantics.
- Pull in `ds-visual-ui-direction` when typography is doing visual heavy lifting for premium feel, hierarchy, or editorial polish.
