## Workflow

1. Treat this as future-facing unless explicitly activated.
2. Keep it separate from the current `Misc` export.
3. Promote it only with clear rules and documentation.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat the current `Misc` token export as the expressive palette.
- Do not merge future creative palette ideas into active system foundations without explicit approval.
- Pull in `ds-visual-ui-direction` when expressive color work must stay tasteful, usable, and system-compatible.
