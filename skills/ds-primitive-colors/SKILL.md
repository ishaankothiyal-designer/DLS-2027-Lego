## Use this skill for

- primitive palette audits
- tonal scale analysis
- new raw color families
- theme palette sourcing

## Workflow

1. Work at the raw palette level only.
2. Keep meaning out of Primitive.
3. Route usage meaning into Semantic or Theme.

## Guardrails

- Do not hardcode Primitive into components.
- Do not turn Primitive into a semantic layer.
- When in doubt, keep palettes shared and map brands in Theme.
- Pull in `ds-visual-ui-direction` when raw palette decisions are being made for visible UI character rather than token plumbing alone.
